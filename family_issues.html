Family Issues

/ here's the thing

All families go through difficult times, and personal relationships will change.
So, how can you deal with family issues?

/ Talk, Accept, and have fun

Communication can help solve so many problems. Accept your differences. Have fun.
Have a dining table chat. Everyone in the family talks about their problems and listens to everyone else's problems. Remember to be calm and honest when discussing.

People have different ideas and opinions, so understand that people will not always be in agreement.

Spend some time with your family, and create unbreakable bonds.

/ Help

Get help from outside the family if necessary.
  • Talk to friends/family
  • Get relationship counseling or mediation
  • Check out online information and resources like https://www.familyrelationships.gov.au/ or Life Line
  • Attend courses/workshops in communication, parenting, budgeting and positive communication skills
  • See your GP if you are concerned about mental health problems in yourself or others
  • Call a helpline like Lifeline or Kids Helpline
